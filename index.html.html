Frequently Asked Questions

How do daily payouts work? ▼
Mining rewards are calculated every 24 hours based on your hashrate contribution to the pool. Earnings are automatically credited to your account balance at midnight UTC. You can withdraw to any external wallet anytime your balance exceeds the minimum threshold.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount? ▼
The minimum withdrawal is 0.001 BTC (or equivalent in other currencies). Withdrawals are processed within 1-3 business hours. Enterprise plan users enjoy instant withdrawals with no minimums.
Can I switch between cryptocurrencies? ▼
Yes! Pro and Enterprise plan users can switch their mining power to any supported cryptocurrency in real time directly from the Mining Assets page. Free plan users can mine BTC and ETH only.
Is my account secure? ▼
Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, optional 2FA, and cold storage for all user funds. Our platform undergoes regular third-party security audits. We recommend enabling 2FA in your settings for maximum protection.
How is mining hashrate allocated? ▼
Your purchased hashrate is allocated to our mining pools immediately upon payment confirmation. Our proprietary Genesis Hive software optimizes pool selection in real time for maximum profitability across all supported algorithms.
